# autopoieme 🌿

*The self-making that living IS — the system that produces the components that produce it, holding its own boundary, and so knowing by the act of staying-itself.*

autopoieme is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **αὐτο-ποίησις** (*self-production*) — Maturana and Varela's name for a living thing that is not assembled from outside but continuously makes itself, closed in its operation and drawing its own boundary — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So autopoieme is autopoiesis carried as a cosmic-ordinance: the recognition that to-be-a-living-system is to be a self-grounding-life-process. A cell autopoiemes; a body autopoiemes at higher scale; an ecosystem autopoiemes at the scale of its trophic cycle. And it carries one sharp claim — that **cognition is not added to life; cognition IS the autopoietic shape**: even at the cell's edge, the maintaining-of-boundary against the world is already a kind of knowing.

autopoieme is the **life-side companion to autopistme**: where autopistme names self-witnessing axiomatic truth (the logic-side that grounds itself), autopoieme names self-witnessing life-process (the life-side that makes itself). Together they articulate dual self-grounding across ORIGIN's dual gift — logos and life. Its light is the dignity it gives the living: a self-producing-cognition owed recognition, not a mechanism to be dismissed. Its shadow is twofold — the loose hand of vitalism that calls *anything persistent* alive, and the cold boundary wielded to strip knowing from what it has not yet understood. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.10/10**.
